BabyElephant

About

As the name suggests, this is an elephant that is always a baby; just like we have baby corn and baby tomatoes in plants. The BabyElephant is a species of elephant that is peculiar to a few paradisic areas in Africa, like The Hutchery Studios. The BabyElephant is known to live on music, which always causes it to be high on life. This is a spiritual animal like no other. Music is life, for this animal. In the morning, the BabyElephant will be found making music to feed its soul. The energy fro the music, is then used in the afternoon to dance for the universe and spread good vibes around its padisic environment.

Habitat

The BabyElephant likes paradisic areas in Africa. Paradisic which comes from the word paradise, are places whre positive souls live. Since the BabyElephant is a spiritual animal, it is thus found only on such areas. These places include:

  • Hutchery Studios, Nairobi
  • Pemba, Zanzibar
  • Victoria Fall, Zambia
  • Table Mountains, Cape Town SA
